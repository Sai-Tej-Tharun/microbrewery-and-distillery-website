Frequently Asked Questions

Walk-ins are always welcome during regular taproom hours. For groups of 6 or more, or if you want a guaranteed table on a busy evening, we recommend booking via the form or calling us directly.

We serve a rotating food menu of small plates and artisan boards that pair beautifully with our brews. Outside food is not permitted in the taproom, but we are happy to accommodate dietary requirements — just let us know in advance.

Beer Club members receive their monthly allocation on the first Tuesday of each month. Deliveries cover the contiguous US. Limited-release notifications go out 48 hours before public sale, giving members first access. You can manage your deliveries in the member dashboard.

Absolutely — well-behaved dogs on leads are welcome in our outdoor beer garden. We even keep a water bowl and dog treats at the bar. Our taproom host Biscuit (a golden retriever) will probably come say hello.

Yes! We offer custom batch brewing for weddings, corporate events, and special occasions. Minimum order is 20 litres. We'll work with you on the recipe, naming, and label design. Enquire via the Private Event form with at least 8 weeks notice.

We supply a select number of independent restaurants and bottle shops across California. We're intentionally small-batch, so availability is limited. Send a wholesale enquiry via the General Contact form selecting "Wholesale" as the subject.
