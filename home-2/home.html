How We Brew

Craft Has No Shortcuts

Every exceptional pour starts with an obsessive process. Here's ours.

01

Malting

We source single-origin barley from California's Central Valley, malted in-house over 5 days for maximum flavour expression.

02

Mashing

Precision temperature control across three-step infusion mashing extracts every last point of fermentable sugar from the grain.

03

Fermentation

Our proprietary yeast strain, cultivated since 2012, drives complex esters and phenols carefully brewed and observed across a slow 14-day ferment.

04

Barrel Aging

Select batches rest in hand-charred American oak and ex-bourbon barrels from 3 months of brewery to 3 years — patience is the only secret ingredient.

05

The Pour

Hand-labeled, cellar-cold, poured fresh at our taproom or sealed in limited air-tight, specialized, dedicated growlers to take a little Mash & Ale home.
