What You'll Learn

Become a More Confident Beer Drinker

Led by our resident Cicerone (the beer equivalent of a sommelier), the masterclass covers the full sensory vocabulary of craft beer — from identifying hop varieties by aroma to understanding how malt profiles affect mouthfeel and bitterness balance.

Classes are capped at 12 guests to ensure an intimate, interactive experience. You'll leave with a printed tasting journal, food pairing guide, and a dramatically better palate.

Session Structure

How the Class Unfolds

A carefully paced 2.5-hour journey designed to inform, challenge, and delight.

0:00–0:20
The Foundations
Brewing ingredients, fermentation science, and the major beer style families — explained clearly and without jargon.
0:20–0:50
Sensory Training
Aroma identification using the kit — hops, malts, esters, and off-flavours. Learning the vocabulary of beer.
0:50–1:50
The Tasting Flight
8 pours spanning lager to imperial stout — guided analysis of each with discussion and note-taking.
1:50–2:30
Food Pairing & Q&A
Pairing demonstrations with a tasting plate, followed by an open Q&A with the Cicerone.
