The Process

A Beer That's Entirely Yours

You don't need to know anything about brewing — that's what we're here for. In an initial consultation, our head brewer will guide you through style, flavour profile, ABV, and naming. We handle every step of the production process.

From there, we'll send a pilot taste sample for your approval before the full batch is brewed. You sign off, we brew, and you receive your beer in custom-labelled bottles or a personal keg.

Timeline

From Brief to Bottle

The full custom brewing journey typically takes 8–12 weeks from initial consultation to delivery.

Week 1–2
Consultation
Recipe brief, style selection, naming, and label concept discussions with your brewer.
Week 3–5
Pilot Batch
We brew a small pilot. You taste, give feedback, and approve before production begins.
Week 6–9
Full Production
The full batch is brewed, conditioned, and quality-checked against your approved pilot.
Week 10–12
Bottling & Delivery
Your beer is bottled with custom labels and ready for collection or delivery.
