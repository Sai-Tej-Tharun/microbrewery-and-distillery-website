What to Expect

Your Tour Journey

Here's what happens from the moment you arrive to your final pour.

Step 01
Arrive & Check In
Meet your brewer guide at the taproom entrance. We'll get you a welcome beer while you wait.
Step 02
The Brew Floor
Walk through the live brewery, exploring mash tuns, boil kettles, and fermenters up close.
Step 03
The Barrel Room
Descend into our barrel aging cellar for a look at our most prized slow-aged ales and stouts.
Step 04
Sit-Down Tasting
Settle into our private tasting room for your 6-pour flight with full tasting notes.
