Our Services

Six Ways to Experience the Craft

Every offering is designed to deepen your connection with the art of brewing — whether you're a curious newcomer or a seasoned hop-head.

01
Guided Experience

Taproom & Cellar Tours

Step behind the bar and into our working cellars for a 90-minute guided journey through the full brewing process — from grain to glass. Includes a 6-pour tasting flight, insider brewer commentary, and exclusive access to barrel rooms not open to the public.

Discover More
02
Private Hire

Private Events & Hire

Transform our taproom or private dining room into the backdrop for your next unforgettable occasion. Birthdays, corporate dinners, weddings, and team events — all supported by bespoke catering, custom beer menus, and a dedicated events coordinator.

Discover More
03
Membership

Beer Club Membership

Join over 8,500 members who receive monthly allocations of seasonal and limited-release brews, first access to new recipes, 15% off taproom purchases, invite-only tasting events, and a loyalty points system that rewards every visit and order.

Discover More
04
Bespoke

Custom Batch Brewing

Collaborate with our head brewer to create an entirely original beer for your wedding, corporate launch, or special occasion. Work together on style, flavour profile, ABV, and a custom label design — producing a minimum 20L batch that's truly yours.

Discover More
05
Trade

Wholesale & Distribution

Supply your restaurant, bar, or bottle shop with our award-winning craft range. We work with a select network of independent venues across California, offering keg and canned lines, flexible MOQs, chilled delivery, and dedicated trade account management.

Discover More
06
Education

Tasting Masterclasses

Learn the language of craft beer in an intimate 2-hour session led by our certified Cicerone. Explore aroma, mouthfeel, bitterness balance, and food pairing across a curated 8-pour flight — perfect for enthusiasts, corporate teams, and gifting occasions.

Discover More
